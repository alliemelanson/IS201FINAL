Who I Was: My High School Marching Band Experience



 

Marching band is a huge deal in Texas. From middle school to high school, I learned how to play the french horn and the mellophone.
In high school, I was a drum major for 2 years, and head drum major my senior year.

Our marching band was nationally renown, taking 1st in our class at nationals and 6th place overall. We were consistent state finalists and medalists.

While I haven't played my instrument in 6 years and marching band is very much over for me, I am forever grateful for the connections I made, the leadership skills I gained, and the opportunities marching band gave me to grow. That community was invaluable to me during highschool, and continues to be a great support to me.
